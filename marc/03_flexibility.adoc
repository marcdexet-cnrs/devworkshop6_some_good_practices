== How to decouple ?

In software, coupling means you're bound by too much knowledge.

* Limit required knowledge
** To be linked to the *WHAT* and not to the *HOW*.
** Use indirection
** Fire and Forget

The WHAT if the promise, the HOW is the implementation.
